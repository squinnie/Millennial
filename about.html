Model of Affect
Valence & Arousal

In general, emotional experiences can be described by two terms:

  • Valence;
  • Arousal.

Though the two terms are often confused, the difference is pretty simple.


Valence is positive or negative affectivity, whereas arousal measures how calming or exciting the information is.
