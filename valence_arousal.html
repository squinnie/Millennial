How to Use

Logging your Affective State


Record your valence and arousal using the LOG feature.

The vertical VALENCE scale ranges from ranges from “unpleasant”(1) to “pleasant”(10).

The horizontal AROUSAL scale ranges from "low arousal/deactivation"(1) to “high arousal/activation”(10).


You may view a summary and breakdown charts of your valence and arousal levels over time.
